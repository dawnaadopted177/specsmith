## Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for:
- Reviewing and merging pull requests
- Triaging issues and security reports
- Releasing new versions
- Maintaining the Agentic AI Development Workflow Specification
- Ensuring CI/CD and dependency health

## Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors who demonstrate sustained, high-quality contributions may be invited to become maintainers. Reach out via GitHub issues or email.
